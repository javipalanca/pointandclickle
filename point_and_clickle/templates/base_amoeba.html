About Point&Clickle

Point&Clickle is a daily game in which you must guess a point and click adventure game every day. You will have 6 chances to get it right by discovering screenshots. If you fail, a new screenshot will be shown. Point&Clickle is the Wordle of adventure games!

About

Point & Clickle is a game for adventure game lovers. It is inspired by Wordle and other variants such as Framed or High Score Day.

Every day a point and click game is randomly selected, and you have 6 attempts to find the game. At each attempt a new frame from the game will be shown.

All images and games have been obtained from Adventure Gamers. All rights go to their respective owners.
